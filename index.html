Know About VIP Aeroservices

VIP Aeroservices is a distributor of consumables, materials, aircraft parts and specialized tools for general and commercial aviation. We manage repair and maintenance projects in aircraft and aeronautical components with the highest quality standards in repair stations (MRO's), aeronautical maintenance organizations (OMA) and workshops certified and authorized by the civil aviation authorities.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Conduct all business affairs with integrity. Defining with pride the values of honesty, truthfulness and sincerity, while remaining fair and ethical. Constantly maintain professional conduct despite facing critical situations. Be ethically uncompromising and honest.

Commitment

Provide our national and international clients with a service they can count on, offering them a positive and memorable experience. Respond constructively and positively to their needs and always keeping our promises.

Accountable

We are accountable to our partners, our team members and ourselves, taking pride in all aspects of the operation, process and work in any business relationship with our current and future clients. We accept constructive feedback as an opportunity to learn and grow. We understand what is expected of us and we are working to exceed our own goals.

Respect

We treat our clients, co-workers, and ourselves with the respect and care we all deserve. We are an honest and open organization that believes in a culture of transparency to inspire trustworthiness. We accept opinions and criticisms to achieve the objectives and goals that we have proposed in this organization.

Excellence

We strive every day to find excellence and we adopt the current changes that are necessary for the improvement of the organization. We also make sure to satisfy all the needs and requirements of the client and go beyond what I originally requested.
