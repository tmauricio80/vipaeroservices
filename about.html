Let’s Introduce About Us

VIP Aeroservices is a distributor of consumables, materials, aircraft parts and specialized tools for general and commercial aviation. We manage repair and maintenance projects in aircraft and aeronautical components with the highest quality standards in repair stations (MRO's), aeronautical maintenance organizations (OMA) and workshops certified and authorized by the civil aviation authorities.

Who We Are

VIP Aeroservices

With our legacy of experience and innovation, VIP Aeroservices is much more than just a company name. The VIP Aeroservices team is a group of people dedicated to serving the aviation community and treating our customers with the respect they deserve. We are a group of people who work as a team to maintain the quality of the products and services that we offer under the banner of VIP Aeroservices.

Mission

Our mission here at VIP Aeroservices is to develop a partnership with our customers to provide maintenance, repairs, parts and materials, with the highest quality standards and services required to support general, private, military and commercial aviation owners and companies.

Vision

VIP Aeroservices is committed to being a leader in support and services for the aviation community and internationally, providing our clients and our team with uncompromising safety, exceptional quality and unmatched customer service.
